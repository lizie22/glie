THE BIG 3

Big Three companies: SM Entertainment, JYP Entertainment, YG Entertainment. They are known for producing the most consistently succesful groups and songs for the Korean and international public.

SM Entertainment

SM Entertainment Co., Ltd. ia a South Korean multinatonal entertainment agency established in 1995 by Lee Soo-man. It is one of South Korea's largest entertainment companies and has been responsible for fostering and popularizing the careers of many K-pop stars garnering huge global fanbases.

JYP Entertainment

JYP Entertainment Corporation is a South Korean multinational entertainment and record label conglomerate founded in 1997 by J.Y.Park.

YG Entertainment

YG Entertainment Inc. is a South Korean multinational entertainment agency established in 1996 by Yang Hyun-suk. The company operates as a recorded label, talent agency, music production company, event management and concert production company, and music publishing house.

Big Three Artists

K-pop idols are characterized by the highly manufactured star system that they are produced by and debuted under, as well as their tendency to represent a hybridized convergence of visuals, music, fashion, and dance.

Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2017 reality show of the same name. The group is composed of eight memebers: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N.

GOT7

Got7 is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of seven members: Jay B, Mark, Jackson, Jinyoung, Youngjae, BamBam, and Yugyeom.

iKON

iKon, stylized as iKON, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2015 by YG Entertainment. The lineup consists of six members: Jay, Song, Bobby, DK, Ju-ne and Chan. Originally a seven-piece band, leader B.I departed from the group in June 2019.

Super Junior

Super Junior, also known as SJ or SuJu, is a South Korean boy band composed of Leeteuk, Heechul, Yesung, Shindong, Sungmin, Eunhyuk, Siwon, Donghae, Ryeowook and Kyuhyun. Han Geng, Kibum, and Kangin were originally part of the group before derparting in 2011, 2015, 2019, respectively.
